## 🛑 The Problem: The "Localization Gap" in Security
Global security giants (Kaspersky, Avast, Norton) are failing in emerging markets because:
1.  **Language Barriers:** They don't recognize fraud patterns in local languages like Uzbek or specific regional dialects.
2.  **Context Ignorance:** They don't track local fintech fraud (e.g., fake Payme/Click portals, Uzcard/Humo verification scams).
3.  **Data Privacy Concerns:** Users are increasingly wary of "security" apps that upload their data to foreign servers for analysis.
4.  **Performance Overload:** Traditional scanners drain batteries and require constant high-speed data—a luxury in many regions.

## 🛠️ The Solution: Cyber Brother PRO
We have built a triple-layered defense system that protects the user where they are most vulnerable:

### 1. **AI-Driven Localized Guard (Accessibility Shield)**
*   **Contextual Analysis:** Real-time monitoring of screen content to detect linguistically specific phishing (e.g., "Karta raqamingizni kiriting" - "Enter your card number" scams).
*   **Vishing Protection:** Detects suspicious call-related activities and warns users during live fraud attempts.
*   **Auto-Gatekeeper:** Intercepts clicks on suspicious links within Telegram, WhatsApp, and SMS before the browser even opens.

### 2. **Privacy-First VPN DNS Shield**
*   **Zero-Data Policy:** Unlike traditional VPNs, our DNS Shield stays 100% on-device.
*   **Malicious Interception:** Blocks known phishing domains and advertising trackers at the DNS level.
*   **VirusTotal Hybrid:** Uses a smart caching system to combine local blacklists with global threat intelligence without compromising speed.

### 3. **Intelligent APK Auditor**
*   **Behavioral Scoring:** Analyzes apps based on their actual permission requests and digital signatures.
*   **Sideloading Blocker:** Monitors installs from unofficial sources (like Telegram channels), which is the #1 vector for malware in Uzbekistan.
